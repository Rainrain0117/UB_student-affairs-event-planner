STEP 1: Create Your Event Letter

 :

Write the letter for the event you want to propose, this serves as a proposal letter that will be evaluated by your mentors, professors, department heads and fellow officers. This should be concise, formal, detailed and contains necessary data to be inscripted for the next step.

STEP 2: Approval from Department Officials

 :

The next step is to let your department officials evaluate and see if the event is possible within your circumstances, they will rephrase reschedule, discuss and finalize the proposal to be passed to Student Affairs and External Programs to be posted for everyone to participate.

STEP 3: Submit Event Proposal to SAEP

 :

The SAEP will review and recommend things to the proposer before it is agreed upon. The SAEP members will give a pending date for it to be announced, it may also reject proposals (not all events are always approved). Once approved, event is set and it can be posted here in the website for students to view.
